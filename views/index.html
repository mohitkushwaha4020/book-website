About the Author

Mohit Kushwaha

Mohit Kushwaha is a storyteller at heart — someone who believes that words, when written from the soul, have the power to heal, connect, and transform.

His books explore the raw, unfiltered emotions of love, loss, longing, and self-discovery. From sweeping romance to deeply introspective self-help, each of his works is a testament to the human experience in all its complexity.

Born with an instinct to observe the quieter moments of life, Mohit writes the stories that live in the spaces between — the unsaid words, the half-finished feelings, and the love that stays long after the person is gone.
